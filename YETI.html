Collaboration with YFS

Our Business Division collaborates with Young Founders Summit (YFS) to provide entrepreneurship opportunities across Asia. Through this partnership, Thai students gain access to:

  • 5-day entrepreneurship bootcamps in Singapore
  • Mentorship from successful business leaders
  • Opportunities to pitch startup ideas to investors
  • A network of young entrepreneurs from across Asia
